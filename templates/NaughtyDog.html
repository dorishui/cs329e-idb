Developer Name: Naughty Dog
Description: Naughty Dog, LLC is an American video game developer based in Santa Monica, California.[5] Founded by Andy Gavin and Jason Rubin in 1984 as an independent developer,[3] the studio was acquired by Sony Computer Entertainment in 2001. Gavin and Rubin produced a sequence of progressively more successful games, including Rings of Power and Way of the Warrior in the early 1990s. The latter – a very low-budget but still plausible offering – prompted Universal Interactive Studios to sign the duo to a three-title contract and fund the expansion of the company.
