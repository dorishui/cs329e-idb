Developer Name: Subset Games
Description: Subset Games was founded by Justin Ma and Matthew Davis, both former 2K China employees. Their first released title was FTL: Faster Than Light which was the product of a successful Kickstarter campaign which raised over 2000% of their requested budget. In February of 2017 Subset Games announced development of a new title Into The Breach.
City: Shanghai
Country: China
